Materials

The tools and supplies we use and recommend for cricket bat repairs.

Bat Repair Glue

A strong wood glue specifically formulated for English willow. Dries clear and forms a flexible bond that handles ball impact.

Fibre Tape

Reinforcement tape applied to the face of the bat to prevent cracks from spreading and to extend the life of the willow.

Cane Handles

Premium cane handles for handle replacement jobs. Available in singapore cane and sarawak cane in multiple grades.

Toe Guard

Rubber toe guard that protects the bottom of the bat from moisture and ground impact. A must-have for new bats.

Linseed Oil

Raw linseed oil for conditioning natural willow. Keeps the wood from drying out and helps prevent cracking over time.

Bat Mallet

A wooden mallet weighted for knocking in new bats. Compresses the willow fibres and prepares the bat for match play.
