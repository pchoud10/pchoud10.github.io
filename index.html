Welcome to CricFix

At CricFix, we believe every cricket bat deserves a second innings. Whether your bat has a hairline crack from a tough session, a worn toe from heavy practice, or a handle that has lost its grip, we have the tools, knowledge, and passion to bring it back to life. Our service combines traditional craftsmanship with modern repair techniques so your favorite bat can keep performing match after match.

How to Fix a Crack

Hairline and surface cracks are common after a heavy season. With wood glue, clamps, and patience, most cracks can be sealed permanently and the bat returned to play.

Toe Repair Guide

The toe takes the most damage from yorkers and ground impact. Trimming, reshaping, and adding a toe guard will dramatically extend the life of your bat.

Handle Replacement

A loose, splintered, or broken handle can be replaced with a new cane handle. This is one of the more advanced repairs, but it can save a bat you thought was finished.

About CricFix

CricFix was established with the simple ideology that your equipment, almost always, deserves a new lease on life. Founded by lifelong cricket fans, we have spent years learning the craft of bat repair from professional bat makers and applying those techniques to club-level cricketers like you. Whether you are a weekend warrior or a college-level batter, we are here to help your willow last longer.
